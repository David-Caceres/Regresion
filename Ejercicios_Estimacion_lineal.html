Using the prostate data, plot lpsa against lcavol. Fit the regressions of lpsa on lcavol and lcavol on lpsa. Display both regression lines on the plot. At what point do the two lines intersect?

##      1.5073       0.7193

## 
## Call:
## lm(formula = lcavol ~ lpsa, data = prostate)
## 
## Coefficients:
## (Intercept)         lpsa  
##     -0.5086       0.7499

Para comparar ambas rectas tenemos que invertir una de ellas, ya que si tomamos la ecuación de la recta, y= a + b * x para la recta1, para la recta2 la x y la y deben intercambiarse, de ese modo:

x = a’ + b’ * y y = (x - a’/b) y = -a’/b’ + 1/b’ * x

plot(lpsa ~ lcavol, data =prostate)
abline(recta1, col="red")
a_2<-recta2$coefficients[1]
b_2<-recta2$coefficients[2]
a2<- -a_2/b_2
b2<- 1/b_2
abline(a2,b2, col="blue")
